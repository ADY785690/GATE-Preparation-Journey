# Normalization PYQs

## PYQ 1

Which Normal Form removes Partial Dependency?

A) 1NF
B) 2NF
C) 3NF
D) BCNF

Answer: ✅ B) 2NF

Why not others?

A) 1NF removes repeating groups

C) 3NF removes transitive dependency

D) BCNF is stricter than 3NF

---

## PYQ 2

Which Normal Form removes Transitive Dependency?

A) 1NF
B) 2NF
C) 3NF
D) BCNF

Answer: ✅ C) 3NF

---

## PYQ 3

BCNF requires:

A) Every attribute must be prime

B) Every determinant must be a Super Key

C) No foreign keys

D) No candidate keys

Answer: ✅ B) Every determinant must be a Super Key

---

## PYQ 4

Which statement is true?

A) Every 3NF relation is BCNF

B) Every BCNF relation is 3NF

C) BCNF is weaker than 3NF

D) 2NF is stronger than BCNF

Answer: ✅ B) Every BCNF relation is 3NF
